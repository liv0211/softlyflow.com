## ✦ Tips for Writing Your Perfect Bio

**1. Lead with feeling, not facts.**
Instead of "Student at NYU | dog mom | coffee addict," try to capture your *energy*. Facts are forgettable; vibes stick.

**2. Use line breaks.**
Instagram bios read faster when they're broken into short lines. Each line should be able to stand alone.

**3. One CTA is enough.**
If you have a link in your bio, one gentle call-to-action ("read my latest ↓") is more elegant than three competing directives.

**4. Update seasonally.**
Your bio doesn't have to be forever. Treat it like a soft-launch of wherever you are right now.

**5. Read it out loud.**
If it sounds like *you*, keep it. If it sounds like a LinkedIn summary, start over.
