## ✦ How to Write Your Own Aesthetic Caption

The best captions don't describe the photo — they add a layer the photo can't show on its own. Try one of these approaches:

**The sensory detail** — What did it smell like? Sound like? Feel like? Bring the reader into the moment beyond what they can see.

**The honest thought** — What were you actually thinking when you took this? The real answer is almost always better than the polished one.

**The single line that stays** — Write ten caption options. The one that sounds most like you on your best day is the one to post.

**The open ending** — End with something that invites the reader to finish the thought in their own mind. Leave a small gap. People fill gaps with themselves, and that's what creates connection.
