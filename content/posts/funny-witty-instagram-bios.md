## ✦ Why Funny Bios Work

A witty bio does something a serious one rarely does: it makes the reader feel something in under three seconds. A genuine laugh, a small recognition, a slight surprise. That micro-reaction is what converts a profile visitor into a follower.

The key is specificity. "Powered by spite and oat milk lattes" is funny because it's oddly specific and somehow universally relatable. "Professional overthinker. Certified snack." works because the contrast between formal language and casual slang creates unexpected friction.

Write ten bad attempts. The eleventh is usually the one.
