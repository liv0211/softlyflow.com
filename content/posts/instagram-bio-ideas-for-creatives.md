Creatives are notoriously bad at writing about themselves. Here are bio ideas so you don't have to.

---

## ✍️ For Writers

- writer. mostly unsent letters.
- turning feelings into sentences since forever
- chronicling the ordinary, beautifully
- words are my love language and my first language
- I write because I can't not
- perpetually mid-draft
- making sense of the world one paragraph at a time
- fiction, essays, and things I write at 2am
- telling stories that deserve to be told
- writing is thinking. I think a lot.

---

## 🎨 For Visual Artists & Illustrators

- making beautiful things in imperfect light
- colour theory and controlled chaos
- illustrating the world as I wish it looked
- sketchbook full, mind fuller
- creating because the blank page is an invitation, not a threat
- art as a daily practice, not a finished product
- my art is my autobiography in colour
- drawing the things I can't say out loud
- everything I feel ends up in paint
- making things by hand in a digital world

---

## 📸 For Photographers

- I photograph what I don't want to forget
- chasing light and finding truth
- documenting the beautiful and the overlooked
- the world is more interesting through a lens
- photography is just formalised looking
- I see a photo in everything (it's a gift and a curse)
- capturing the feeling, not just the scene
- every click is a conversation with the moment
- I photograph people as they are, not as they think they should be
- the camera taught me to pay attention

---

## 🏺 For Makers & Craftspeople

- making things slowly and with care
- hands in clay / thread / wood / [your medium]
- building things that last
- slow craft in a fast world
- every piece is made with intention
- making by hand because it matters
- the process is the point
- creating objects that deserve to be touched
- I make things because I need to
- craft as a form of meditation

---

## 🎵 For Musicians

- music is my first language
- chords, words, and everything in between
- I write songs for the feelings that don't have names
- making sound into meaning
- the song is finished when it says what I meant
- music kept me whole when nothing else did
- playing in rooms of various sizes
- the melody arrived before the words, as usual
- sound is the most invisible art form and I'm obsessed with it
- composing my way through existence

---

## 🌿 General Creative Bios

- building something from nothing every day
- creating as a way of making sense of things
- making things is how I know I'm alive
- creativity is not a mood — it's a practice
- art is how I survive everything
- I make things. sometimes people like them.
- perpetually mid-project and entirely okay with it
- creating the things I couldn't find
- documenting this one wild creative life
- if you're not making something, what are you doing

---
