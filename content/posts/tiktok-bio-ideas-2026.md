## ✦ TikTok Bio Formatting Tips

**Keep it under 80 characters** — TikTok cuts off anything longer on mobile.

**One emoji max** — two or more looks cluttered on a small screen.

**Skip your age and location** — nobody needs to know unless it's part of your brand.

**Add a CTA only if you have a link** — "link below ↓" with nothing there looks worse than nothing.

**Update it seasonally** — a stale bio signals an inactive account.
